# AI and Culture

This repository is dedicated to exploring the intersection of artificial intelligence and culture. The goal is to facilitate discussions, share resources, and document projects that highlight the impact of AI on various cultural aspects.

## Overview
- Examination of AI's role in cultural production.
- Impact on arts, literature, and media.
- Ethical considerations surrounding AI in cultural contexts.

To get started with the projects in this repository, please reference the documentation available for each project.
